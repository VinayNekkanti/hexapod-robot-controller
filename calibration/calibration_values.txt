## Notes

- Typical range: 80-100 degrees for neutral positions
- Small adjustments (Â±5-10 degrees) are normal
- If a servo requires >20 degree adjustment, check mechanical assembly
- Re-calibrate if you disassemble/reassemble the robot

## Troubleshooting

**Servo doesn't move:**
- Check power supply
- Verify I2C connections (SDA to A4, SCL to A5)
- Ensure servo is properly connected to PCA9685

**Servo jitters:**
- Add capacitor across power rails
- Check battery voltage (should be >7.0V)
- Reduce load on servo

**Inconsistent positions:**
- Servo may be damaged - replace if needed
- Check for mechanical binding
- Ensure adequate power supply current
